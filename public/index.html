About Reef Chinuch

Reef Chinuch Restaurant It is a Costa Rican restaurant located on the road to the Arenal volcano, we have more than 20 years of experience in Culinary Art.

At Reef Chinuch Restaurant we specialize in typical foods from different countries, especially Costa Rica, among which the typical foods of Peru, Canada and Italy also stand out.
